Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a fantastic, simple to make dish made up of healthy ingredients. Guacamole is perfect when paired with chips as an appetizer, it also works well as a topping to many of your favorite Mexican inspired dishes.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Roma Tomatoes
  • Cilantro
  • Minced Garlic
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps

  1. Scoop out Avocados
  2. Mash Avocados for 3 minutes
  3. Add remaining ingredients and mash for another 3 minutes
  4. Serve and enjoy!
